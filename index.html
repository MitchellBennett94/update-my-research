UpdateMyResearch is an open-source website, where scientists can collect, store, and manage their research data efficiently and collaboratively.

Check out the toolkit features:

  • Data Organization: Structure and categorize your research data for easy access and retrieval.
  • Collaboration Tools: Share data and collaborate with team members in real-time.
  • Version Control: Track changes and maintain different versions of your datasets.
  • Metadata Management: Add descriptive metadata to enhance data discoverability.
  • Data Sharing: Facilitate secure data sharing with external collaborators or the public.
